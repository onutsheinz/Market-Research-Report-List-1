<p><p>The building insulation market comprises several key types. Stone or rock wool, made from natural rock, offers excellent thermal and acoustic insulation and fire resistance. Glass wool, created from recycled glass, provides lightweight insulation with good thermal properties. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are rigid foam insulations, with EPS being cost-effective and lightweight, while XPS offers superior moisture resistance and insulation performance. Other types include cellulose and foam boards, catering to specific insulation needs in construction.</p></p>
