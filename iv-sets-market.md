<p><p>IV sets are essential tools in medical settings, categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Filtered IV sets contain filters to remove impurities, ensuring sterile fluid delivery. Vented IV sets include a venting mechanism that allows air to enter the IV bag, maintaining consistent flow without vacuum formation. Non-vented IV sets do not require air vents, relying on pressure differences for fluid delivery. Other IV sets may encompass specialized designs for unique medical requirements, enhancing overall treatment efficiency.</p></p>
