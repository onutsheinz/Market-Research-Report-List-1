<p><p>The heavy-duty trucks market is diverse, comprising various types designed for specific tasks. Tractor-trailers, also known as semis, consist of a tractor unit and a detachable trailer, ideal for long-haul freight. Tank trucks transport liquids, including fuels and chemicals, with specialized tanks for safe delivery. Haul trucks are designed for heavy-duty mining and construction environments, capable of carrying large loads across rugged terrains.</p></p>
