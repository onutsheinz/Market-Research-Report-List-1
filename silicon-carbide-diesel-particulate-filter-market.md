<p><strong>The Silicon Carbide Diesel Particulate Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Pssive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Carbide Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) market is primarily divided into active and passive types. Active DPFs utilize heating elements to facilitate the regeneration process, enabling the removal of accumulated soot during lower temperature operations. This technology is particularly beneficial for vehicles with low exhaust temperatures. Conversely, passive DPFs rely on exhaust temperatures to promote automatic regeneration, making them more suited for vehicles that consistently operate at higher temperatures. Both types aim to enhance emissions control in diesel engines.</p></p>
