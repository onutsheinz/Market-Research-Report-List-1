<p><p>The non-invasive sphygmomanometer market comprises three main types: automatic sphygmomanometers, mercury sphygmomanometers, and aneroid sphygmomanometers. Automatic sphygmomanometers provide digital readings and are user-friendly, making them popular for home and clinical use. Mercury sphygmomanometers offer precise measurements but are increasingly restricted due to health and environmental concerns. Aneroid sphygmomanometers use a dial and manual techniques for blood pressure measurement, appealing for their accuracy and portability, widely used in clinical settings and by healthcare professionals.</p></p>
