<p><p>Outdoor warning sirens are classified into three main types based on their sound projection capabilities. Directional sirens emit sound in a specific direction, making them effective for targeted alerts. Rotating sirens utilize a rotating mechanism to distribute sound across a broader area but can lose intensity over distance. Omni-directional sirens disperse sound evenly in all directions, ensuring widespread coverage. Each type serves unique needs in emergency warning systems, catering to varying geographical and urban conditions.</p></p>
