<p><p>The silicon nitride target market comprises two primary types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, static surfaces used in processes like sputtering for thin-film deposition, providing uniform coatings. In contrast, rotating targets feature a cylindrical design that allows for continuous movement during the deposition process, enhancing material utilization and efficiency. Both types cater to various applications in electronics, optics, and ceramics, with demand driven by advancements in technology and the need for high-performance materials.</p></p>
