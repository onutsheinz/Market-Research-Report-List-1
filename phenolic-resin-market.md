<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Phenolic Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resol Resin</li><li>Novolac Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phenolic resin market comprises primarily two types: resol resin and novolac resin. Resol resin is produced through a one-step reaction and is thermosetting, making it suitable for applications requiring heat resistance and durability, such as in adhesives and coatings. In contrast, novolac resin is a thermosetting phenolic resin formed through a two-step process, typically used in molding applications and as a binder for other materials. Both types are widely utilized in industries like automotive, construction, and electrical components.</p></p>
