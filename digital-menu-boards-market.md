<p><p>Digital menu boards are categorized primarily into two market types: on-premises and cloud-based systems. On-premises digital menu boards are installed directly at the location, offering businesses full control over their content and local network management. In contrast, cloud-based systems enable remote access and updates, allowing for real-time changes without physical presence. This flexibility facilitates seamless content management across multiple locations, enhancing operational efficiency and enabling quick adaptations to menu offerings or promotional strategies in response to customer preferences.</p></p>
