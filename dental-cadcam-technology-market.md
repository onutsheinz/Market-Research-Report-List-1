<p><strong>The Dental CAD/CAM Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Milling Machines</li><li>Furnaces</li><li>Integrated Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental CAD/CAM technology encompasses three main types: milling machines, furnaces, and integrated systems. Milling machines are used to create precise dental restorations from various materials, ensuring high accuracy and efficiency. Furnaces are essential for sintering dental materials, enhancing their strength and durability. Integrated systems combine software and hardware, streamlining the entire dental workflow from design to production. Together, these types improve productivity and quality in dental practices, enabling faster and more accurate creation of crowns, bridges, and other dental restorations.</p></p>
