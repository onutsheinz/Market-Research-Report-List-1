<p><strong>The Multi-chip Module (MCM) Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MCM-D</li><li>MCM-C</li><li>MCM-L</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-chip Module (MCM) Packaging market consists of three primary types: MCM-D, MCM-C, and MCM-L. MCM-D (Device) integrates multiple semiconductor chips within a single package, emphasizing high-density interconnections. MCM-C (Ceramic) utilizes ceramic materials for improved electrical performance and thermal stability, often suited for high-reliability applications. MCM-L (Laminated) employs laminated substrates, allowing for flexible designs and enhanced thermal management. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing performance, size, and reliability in advanced electronic systems.</p></p>
