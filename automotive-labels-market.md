<p><p>The automotive labels market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere easily with minimal application effort, making them widely used for branding and information. Glue-applied labels rely on adhesive for attachment, offering durability. Heat transfer labels are printed onto substrates using heat, ensuring a smooth finish and longevity. In-mold labels are integrated during the manufacturing process, enhancing durability and aesthetics. Other label types encompass specialty applications, including RFID tags and customized designs, catering to diverse automotive needs.</p></p>
