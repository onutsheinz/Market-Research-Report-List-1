<p><p>The dental implant abutment market is primarily categorized into straight and angled abutments. Straight abutments provide a direct connection between the implant and the crown, ideal for aligned dental placements. In contrast, angled abutments are designed for cases where the implant is positioned at an angle, allowing for better crown placement and aesthetic alignment. Both types are essential for enhancing the functionality and appearance of dental restorations, catering to varying patient needs and implant positioning scenarios.</p></p>
