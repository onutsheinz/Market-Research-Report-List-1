<p><strong>The Phased Array Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Phased Array Antenna</li><li>Passive Phased Array Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phased array antenna market encompasses two primary types: active and passive phased array antennas. Active phased array antennas incorporate individual amplifiers for each antenna element, allowing for precise beam steering and improved performance across various frequencies, making them ideal for advanced applications like radar and telecommunications. Conversely, passive phased array antennas rely on a single transmitter and do not amplify the signal at each element, resulting in simpler design and lower costs, often used for traditional broadcasting and surveillance applications.</p></p>
