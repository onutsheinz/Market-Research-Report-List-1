<p><p>The lighting market for Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) and Electric Vehicles (EVs) encompasses three main types: Halogen, LED, and Xenon lighting. Halogen lighting is traditional, offering affordable solutions but lower efficiency. LED lighting, increasingly popular, provides superior energy efficiency, longevity, and brightness while enabling advanced features like adaptive lighting. Xenon, or HID lighting, delivers bright, white illumination, enhancing visibility but generally at higher costs. Together, these technologies cater to diverse consumer preferences and enhance vehicle safety and aesthetics.</p></p>
