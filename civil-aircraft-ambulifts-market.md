<p><ul><li>SideBull</li><li>FrontBull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Civil Aircraft Ambulifts market is categorized into two main types: SideBull and FrontBull ambulifts. SideBull ambulifts are designed to approach aircraft from the side, allowing for efficient patient loading and unloading directly from the cabin door. In contrast, FrontBull ambulifts operate from the front, enabling access to the cockpit area or front passenger doors. Both types are essential for providing rapid medical assistance on airfields, ensuring that patients receive timely care, while optimizing operational logistics.</p></p>
