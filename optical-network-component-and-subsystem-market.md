<p><p>The optical network component and subsystem market is categorized into active components, passive components, and optical modules. Active components include devices like transceivers and amplifiers that require power to function and enable signal processing. Passive components, such as splitters and couplers, do not need external power and manage signal routing and distribution. Optical modules encompass integrated assemblies that house active and passive components, facilitating data transmission and connectivity in optical networks. Together, they enhance network performance and efficiency.</p></p>
