<p><p>The commercial central air conditioning market comprises several types, each catering to different needs. Water-cooled air conditioning systems utilize water for heat exchange, making them efficient for larger buildings. Multi-split packaged air conditioning allows multiple indoor units to connect to a single outdoor unit, offering flexibility and zone control. Single-split packaged air conditioning features one indoor and one outdoor unit, ideal for smaller spaces. Other systems may include variable refrigerant flow (VRF) and rooftop units, providing additional options for diverse commercial applications.</p></p>
