<p><p>The Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride (uPVC) market is primarily divided into two types: modified uPVC and unmodified uPVC. Modified uPVC includes additives to enhance properties like impact resistance and UV stability, making it suitable for more demanding applications such as windows and doors. Unmodified uPVC, on the other hand, maintains its original chemical structure, providing durability and resistance to corrosion, making it ideal for general construction and plumbing uses. Both types play crucial roles across various industries.</p></p>
