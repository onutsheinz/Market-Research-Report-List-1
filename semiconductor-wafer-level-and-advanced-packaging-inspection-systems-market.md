<p><p>The Semiconductor Wafer-level and Advanced Packaging Inspection Systems market is categorized into Optical Based and Infrared Type systems. Optical Based systems utilize visible light for high-resolution imaging and defect detection, making them ideal for assessing surface quality and fine features on wafers. Infrared Type systems, on the other hand, leverage infrared light to inspect materials and structures that may not be visible to standard optical methods, aiding in thermal analysis and identifying issues in packaging layers. Both types are essential for ensuring product reliability and performance.</p></p>
