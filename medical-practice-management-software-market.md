<p><p>Medical Practice Management Software (MPMS) can be categorized into three main types: web-based, on-premise, and cloud-based solutions. Web-based software operates through internet browsers, allowing easy access from any device while eliminating local installation. On-premise software is installed directly on a facility's hardware, providing control but requiring maintenance and updates by staff. Cloud-based solutions leverage remote servers for data storage and processing, offering flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs, making them suitable for diverse healthcare environments.</p></p>
