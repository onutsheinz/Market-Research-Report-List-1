<p><p>The Low Pressure Reverse Osmosis Membrane Market primarily features two types: Composite Membranes and Asymmetric Membranes. Composite membranes consist of multiple layers that enhance filtration efficiency and provide durability, making them suitable for a range of applications. In contrast, asymmetric membranes possess a non-uniform porosity structure, allowing for selective permeability and facilitating high flow rates while minimizing fouling. Both types are essential in various industries, including water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, due to their effectiveness in separating contaminants from liquids.</p></p>
