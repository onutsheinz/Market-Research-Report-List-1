<p><p>The impact-resistant glass market features various interlayer types that enhance safety and durability. Ionoplast polymer interlayers offer superior strength and tear resistance, suitable for high-impact environments. Each interlayer type contributes unique properties, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
