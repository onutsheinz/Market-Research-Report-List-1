<p><p>The electronic pulsator market is primarily divided into two types based on the number of exits: 4-exit pulsators and 2-exit pulsators. The 4-exit pulsator features four connections for milking multiple teats simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and reducing milking time for larger herds. Conversely, the 2-exit pulsator is designed for smaller operations with two teat connections, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution. Both types play a crucial role in modern dairy farming, improving productivity and animal welfare.</p></p>
