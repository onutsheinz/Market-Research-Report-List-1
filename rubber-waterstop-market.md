<p><strong>The Rubber Waterstop Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dumbbell Type</li><li>Center Bulb Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber waterstop market is primarily divided into two types: dumbbell type and center bulb type. The dumbbell type features a simple, elongated shape with bulbous ends, providing effective sealing in construction joints. This design ensures maximum flexibility and easy installation. In contrast, the center bulb type has a central bulge that allows for better hydraulic performance and resistance to high water pressure. Both types are essential for preventing water ingress in structures like dams, tunnels, and basements.</p></p>
