<p><strong>The Safety Belt Alarm Harness Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slide Version</li><li>Mechanical Version</li><li>Hall Version</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Safety Belt Alarm Harness Market can be categorized into three main types: Slide Version, Mechanical Version, and Hall Version. The Slide Version incorporates sliding mechanisms that trigger alarms when seatbelts are unfastened. The Mechanical Version relies on traditional mechanical components to detect seatbelt status and activate alerts. The Hall Version employs hall-effect sensors to monitor magnetized seatbelt closures, providing a more advanced detection method. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to varying safety regulations and consumer preferences.</p></p>
