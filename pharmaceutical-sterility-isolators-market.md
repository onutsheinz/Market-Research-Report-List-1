<p><ul><li>Sterility Test Isolator</li><li>Sterility Production Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical sterility isolators market comprises two main types: sterility test isolators and sterility production isolators. Sterility test isolators are designed to perform sterility testing of pharmaceutical products in a controlled environment, ensuring no contamination occurs during testing. Conversely, sterility production isolators are utilized during the manufacturing process to maintain aseptic conditions, preventing microbial contamination of sterile products. Both types are essential for ensuring product integrity and compliance with stringent regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
