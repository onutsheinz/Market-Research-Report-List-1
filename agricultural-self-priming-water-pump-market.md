<p><ul><li>ZW Type Self-Priming Pump</li><li>QW Type Self-Priming Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Agricultural Self-priming Water Pump market includes two main types: ZW Type and QW Type self-priming pumps. ZW Type pumps are designed for general agricultural applications, effectively handling dirty water with suspended solids, making them suitable for drainage and irrigation tasks. In contrast, QW Type pumps are optimized for dewatering and can efficiently handle wastewater, featuring enhanced wear resistance. Both types facilitate easy operation without an external water source, providing reliability and efficiency for farmers in various irrigation and drainage applications.</p></p>
