<p><p>An automotive gear pump is a type of hydraulic pump commonly used in vehicles to transfer fluids such as oil, fuel, or cooling liquids. These pumps are integral to a vehicle’s power steering and transmission systems due to their reliability and efficiency.</p><p>The Automotive Gear Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.5% during the forecast period.
