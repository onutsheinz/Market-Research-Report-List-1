<p><p>The off-road motorcycle helmets market comprises three primary types: ABS, Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GFRP), and Carbon Fiber. ABS helmets are affordable and provide decent impact resistance, making them popular among casual riders. GFRP helmets offer a balance of strength and weight, enhancing durability and comfort. Carbon Fiber helmets are the lightest and strongest option, providing superior protection and often featuring advanced aerodynamics, appealing to serious enthusiasts and competitive riders for higher performance and safety.</p></p>
