<p><ul><li>Cold Drawn</li><li>Hot Rolled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel seamless tube market is primarily divided into two main types: cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn tubes are manufactured by pulling heated steel through a die, resulting in improved dimensional accuracy, surface finish, and mechanical properties. They are commonly used in applications requiring high precision. Conversely, hot rolled tubes are produced by heating steel above its recrystallization temperature, making them easier to shape but generally less precise. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
