<p><strong>The Straw Briquetting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li><li>Screw Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The straw briquetting machine market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, hydraulic, and screw extrusion. Mechanical machines utilize a pressing mechanism to compress straw into briquettes, offering efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Hydraulic machines leverage hydraulic pressure for higher density and stronger briquettes, ideal for large-scale production. Screw extrusion machines use a screw conveyor to push straw through a die, enabling continuous operation and the creation of various briquette shapes. Each type addresses different production needs and material characteristics.</p></p>
